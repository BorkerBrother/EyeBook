    An eyebook is a type of book that is designed to be listened and read. Each page containing a different sound or ambience. Eyebooks can be used for a variety of purposes, from educational materials to entertainment. One of the benefits of an eyebook is that it can engage listeners in a unique and interactive way. Unlike traditional books, eyebooks can incorporate sound effects, music, and other audio elements to enhance the reading experience.
